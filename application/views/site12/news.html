🪷 JAGDAMB JAGDAMB — Grand Festival Celebration

📅 2025  ·  📍 Aai Tulja Bhavani Mandir, Paras

The grand JAGDAMB JAGDAMB festival was celebrated with immense devotion and grandeur at Aai Tulja Bhavani Mandir, Paras. Thousands of devotees gathered to offer prayers, participate in the aarti, and seek the blessings of Maa Bhavani. The event was marked by colourful decorations, bhajans, kirtans, and a serene atmosphere of spiritual joy. The Sadhana Foundation committee worked tirelessly to ensure a smooth and divine celebration for all attendees.
