Sree Mookkuthala Bhagavathy Kshetram

The famous Sanskrit scholar and poet Sri Melpathur Bhattathiripad after writing Narayaneeyam and getting cured of his rheumatic disease, came to Mookkuthala for worship- ping the Devi to attain salvation on the advice of Lord Guruvayurappa.
